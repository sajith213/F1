System Overview

The Fuel management system is organized into several key modules:

  • POS: Point of sale system for processing fuel and product sales
  • Pump Management: Monitoring and maintenance of fuel pumps
  • Tank Management: Fuel storage tank monitoring and management
  • Credit Management: Customer credit account management
  • Cash Settlement: Daily cash reconciliation and reporting
  • Price Management: Product and fuel price management
  • Fuel Ordering: Inventory management and supplier interaction
  • Reports: Business analytics and reporting
  • Staff/Attendance: Employee management and attendance tracking

Technical Details

The application appears to be built using:

  • PHP backend with MySQL database
  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript frontend
  • Organized in a modular structure for maintainability
  • Includes both web interface and API endpoints
  • Includes backup functionality for data protection
